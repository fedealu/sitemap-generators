#summary A list of discussion groups with webmasters using sitemaps.org Sitemap files

# Introduction #

The following is a list of the discussion groups where webmasters can share questions and information about Sitemap files, generators and other related issues. Some groups also contain general sub-groups about other webmaster related items. For complex issues, it may be worthwhile to post in the English group as well as in your local one.
